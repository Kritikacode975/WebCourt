PROCESS OF SYSTEM

1
CASE INTAKE

The process of capturing and recording new case information, including case type, parties involved, and initial details.

2
ASSIGNMENT

Assigning cases to specific users or teams within the system based on predefined criteria or manual assignment by an administrator.

3
MANAGEMENT

Managing cases through various stages of the legal process, including tracking case progress, updating case details, and managing associated documents and communications.

4
REPORTING

Generating reports and analyzing data to track key metrics, monitor case trends, and identify areas for improvement in case management processes.
